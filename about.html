Our History

From Construction to Enterprise

Started in 2015 as H&H Construction, we quickly made a name for ourselves in Fayette, Pennsylvania, by simply doing what we said we would do.

In 2023, we rebranded to H&H Enterprise to reflect our expanding community outlook on modern interior design and technology. While our name changed, our core belief remains: we are a humble, honest company. We want to make a fair living, not rob people blind. We genuinely care about our customers, prioritizing your satisfaction above all else.
